So why only watch heat? ↓

the judgment

Do not monitor everything. Monitor only what the AI cannot fix.

These robots are closed-loop: they see the object and adapt, so they shrug off most hardware variation. Run every candidate fault through two filters, and watch the list collapse to almost nothing.

Filter 1: can the closed-loop policy compensate? Filter 2: does it actually recur on this hardware? Burden of proof is on inclusion. One fault survives with hard evidence: motor thermal.
