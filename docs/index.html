Predictive maintenance, per joint

End of day, the same registers feed a CUSUM drift detector for each joint, on two channels: thermal (temperature) and effort (reference-motion current). A joint is flagged only when the trend both drifts statistically and degrades meaningfully. Watch a joint climb, see where maintenance was called (drift detected), and where it became too bad to operate (the action cap).
